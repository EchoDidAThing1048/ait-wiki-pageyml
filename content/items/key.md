## What keys are there?

* Iron
* Gold
* Netherite
* Classic

![The keys](images/keys/key/keys.png)

## What do they do?

Everything after iron unlocks snapping if you have enough loyalty for it,each rank of key increases its range aside iron which doesn't have the ability to snap.
